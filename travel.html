Travel Tips

  • Get a SiM Card with internet access :Be it for accessing Google maps or booking tasks and oredring food online, a local SIM Card is the best solution for nonstop internet access.
  • Pack Light:We don't have stress over packing, even for a long India trips.
  • Stay Safe: Keep your valuables in a safe and be aware of your surroundings.
  • Book in Advance: Book flights and accommodation early to get the best deals.
  • Beaware of Tourists scams: Always opt for travel insurance for peace of mind.
  • Keep a mask while travelling:Wearing a face mask protect you from viruses.
  • Practice good hygiene :Undertake appropriate hand and respiratory hygiene, and physically distance from others while travelling
